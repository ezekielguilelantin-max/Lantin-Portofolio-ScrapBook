My Personal Life

I was born in Pampanga in 2008, but my life changed when my family moved to Quezon City. Growing up in a new city was challenging, but I quickly adjusted. I started my education at Quirino Elementary School, where I studied from kindergarten to Grade 6. That school became my second home, and I made a lot of memories and friends.

After elementary, I transferred to Don Quintin Calderon High School for Grades 7 and 8. The experience was different, and I learned to adapt to new environments and people. Then I moved to Tierra Monte School for Grade 9, and later transferred to NCBA for Grade 10. Unfortunately, I had to stop studying for one year, which was a hard time for me. But I didn't give up.

Now, I am continuing my journey at Quirino High School, and I chose the TVL ICT strand. This is my story — it taught me the value of perseverance, growth, and following what I'm passionate about.

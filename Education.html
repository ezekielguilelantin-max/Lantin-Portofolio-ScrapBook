Education

My academic journey from Pampanga to Quezon City

My Academic Story

I was born in Pampanga in 2008, but my life changed when my family moved to Quezon City. Growing up in a new city was challenging, but I quickly adjusted. I started my education at Quirino Elementary School, where I studied from kindergarten to Grade 6. That school became my second home, and I made a lot of memories and friends.

After elementary, I transferred to Don Quintin Calderon High School for Grades 7 and 8. Then I moved to Tierra Monte School for Grade 9, and later transferred to NCBA for Grade 10. Unfortunately, I had to stop studying for one year — a hard time — but I didn't give up.

Now, I am continuing my journey at Quirino High School with the TVL ICT strand. I chose this because I really want to learn more about computers and technology. I'm excited to explore skills like programming, troubleshooting, and web development.

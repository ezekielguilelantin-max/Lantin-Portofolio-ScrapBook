My Family Background

I was born in Pampanga in 2008, but my life changed when my family moved to Quezon City. Growing up in a new city was challenging, but I quickly adjusted. I started my education at Quirino Elementary School, where I studied from kindergarten to Grade 6. That school became my second home, and I made a lot of memories and friends.

After elementary, I transferred to Don Quintin Calderon High School for Grades 7 and 8. The experience was different, and I learned to adapt to new environments and people. Then I moved to Tierra Monte School for Grade 9, and later transferred to NCBA for Grade 10.

My family has always been my foundation — their support and love kept me going through every challenge and transition. This collection of photos captures some of our most cherished moments together.
